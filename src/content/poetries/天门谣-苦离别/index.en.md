## 原文

* 风啸留时意，卷沙影、后尘不定。
  
  西望去，更多情踌躇！
  
* 四月乱花灼灼似火， 玉管石琴新演曲。
  
  孰共奏？
  
  泪水苦，离别更苦！

## AI Translation

* The howling wind lingers with intent, Rolling up sand and shadows, the dust unsettled.

  Gazing westward, more affection lingers in hesitation!

* In April, the chaotic flowers blaze like fire, The jade flute and stone zither play a new tune.
  
  Who will play together?
  
  Bitter are the tears, but parting is even more bitter!
