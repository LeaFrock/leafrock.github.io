## Source Text

看似是一次，

其实无数次。

看似有的选，

其实没得选。

人就是这样被牵着，

走向命运的终点。

甚至想不出，

可以留的遗言。

## AI Translation

*Please translate my creation into an English poem.*

It seems but once,

Yet countless times.

It seems there’s choice,

Yet none to choose.

Thus led by strings,

To fate’s end.

No words to leave,

In life’s descent.
